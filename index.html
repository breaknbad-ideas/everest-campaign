Amplification
Additional Campaign Ideas
Highly recommended extensions that maximize reach, build anticipation, and create ongoing engagement beyond the core moments.
Idea 01

"Road to Everest" Series

Short episodic content leading up to the jump: altitude training, packing the cards, travel to Nepal. Builds narrative and anticipation vs. a one-off moment.

Series Content
Idea 02

Altitude Countdown Mechanic

Visual countdown from sea level to 29,000 ft. Each post represents increasing altitude milestones. Hook: "We're getting closer to the highest break ever."

Visual Hook
Idea 03

Card Reveal Strategy

Tease the cards before the stunt with blurred previews, partial reveals, and "Guess the card" engagement posts. Drives collector speculation and shares.

Engagement
Idea 04

"What Would You Risk?"

Engagement-driven social prompt: "Would you skydive with a $X card?" Polls, duets, and stitches that invite audience participation.

Social Prompt
Idea 05

Live Stream Tie-In

Pre-jump live stream Q&A to build hype. Post-jump reaction stream breaking down the footage. Brings the community into the moment in real time.

Live
Idea 06

Limited "Everest Break" Drop

If feasible with Fanatics: special branded packs tied to the stunt. Only available during the campaign window. Scarcity drives action.

Commerce
Idea 07

Press + PR Angle

Pitch as "First-ever sports card break above Mount Everest." Targets sports media, collectibles outlets, and mainstream viral coverage.

PR
Idea 08

"Did It Survive?" Post-Stunt Hook

After landing: reveal the condition of the cards. Adds suspense and payoff. The question everyone will be asking, turned into content.
